About Me

I was born in Wyoming and went to school in Pennsylvania, and I have a background in
Psychology and Engineering. From a very early age, I have been obsessed with
figuring out how everything works from bikes to computers to the human brain.

I am now a web developer with experience building single page web apps using Ruby on Rails
and React/Flux as well as browser games using JavaScript/jQuery/CSS/HTML5. I am a natural
problem solver and find great pleasure in tackling the big problems. I am currently located in New York City.

In my free time, I enjoy playing guitar, reading, and being outdoors. I also love to meet new people so if you
have any questions/comments feel free to contact me (above) or check out my resume (below).
